About Motorsport Manager

Motorsport Manager is a racing management-simulation strategy video game series developed by British video game developer, Playsport Games.

This series is made up of 3 entries,

Motorsport Manager

Motorsport Manager Mobile 2

Motorsport Manager Mobile 3

In the games, players take on the role of a team principal, managing a motorsport team with the ablility to hire or fire anyone they see fit and making the difficult decisions to lead them to victory.
